DSW Cutting

Brand site for a 230,000 sq ft fab shop. The customer-facing marketing + capabilities site for DSW — the shop where I engineer by day. Full capability spec, five industry-specific use cases, clear quoting flow.

DSW is a precision metal fab shop in Birmingham — 230,000 square feet, 20+ years in, lasers and tube lasers and a robotic weld cell in the back. I designed and built the customer-facing site so a plant manager at an 18-wheeler OEM can figure out in 90 seconds whether we can make their part.

Each capability tile leads with the numbers engineers actually care about — 2″ max plate, 40 ft tube outbound, 250 ton brake, four OTC DAIHEN robots. The featured-work blocks pair material (14GA CR steel, A36 plate, structural tube) with process (laser cut, plasma bevel, robotic bend) with proof (PPAP, FARO arm CMM, 6,200+ parts a month).

Industries split out so readers can sort themselves. The whole point is to shorten the first sales call.
