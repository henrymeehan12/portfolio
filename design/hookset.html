Hookset Projects

Running a brand. A fishing brand with real shirts, real articles, and a real Shopify store. Alabama Hydrological Map Tee. Cahaba River Collection. A YouTube channel. Built from scratch; still running.

Hookset is the water side of my life. After six seasons guiding, the fishing days slowed down once I started engineering. Hookset was how I kept a foot in the river — a brand for people who fish, camp, and actually care about the specific water they live near.

The product line leans Southern. There's a Cahaba River Collection with four variants (fishing, lily, NWR, scene), a Tired Camper collection, an Alabama Hydrological Map tee that's basically an info-design piece I made because I wanted one, and Trout T's for the fly crowd.

The content side is as much of the brand as the product — long articles like "America's Amazon" on Alabama's river systems, a photography blog, and a YouTube channel I'm still figuring out. The goal is a fishy little community with tribal knowledge. Good stories, decent gear, nobody taking themselves too seriously.
