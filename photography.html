01 · Montana, 2019–2024

The Madison River

In 2017 I went on my first fly fishing trip to Montana with Lillard Fly Fishing Expeditions. I went back every summer. In 2019 Will Lillard asked if I'd guide for him. I guided every summer until 2025. Six seasons on the best trout river I've ever fished — and eventually, teaching kids to fish it for the first time.

02 · Wyoming Backcountry

Yellowstone Drainage

We packed a raft in to reach water that doesn't get fished. Flooded meadows, grizzly tracks in the sand that morning. Yellowstone cutthroat in the headwaters — orange as a goldfish, fat as a football. The hike back out nearly killed us.

03 · New Zealand, Early 2024

Back Country

First stop on a four-month trip. Back country rivers in the South Island — no trails, fish that had probably never seen a fly. I'd been chasing this water since 2017. The trout here are beyond what the word "trout" prepares you for.

04 · Fiji · Singapore · Thailand · Vietnam, 2024

Ha Giang & Beyond

The Ha Giang Loop is four days of mountain roads in the far north of Vietnam — passes, karst towers, villages in clouds. There's a cave on the Nho Que River where you take a boat in the dark and the stalactites come down to the water. I spent a long time in that cave.

05 · Japan, April 2024

Attention

Japan rewards attention. Not the temples — though Fushimi Inari at dawn is everything — but the single tea cup, the train platform at 6am, the way Fuji appears between two buildings if you're looking at the right angle. And the food. I didn't eat badly once.

06 · Nevada · Utah · Texas, October 2024

Road Trip South

I drove from Montana back to Alabama in October. Went through Nevada, the Bonneville Salt Flats, Moab, Big Bend. Didn't hurry. You don't understand how big this country is until you drive it without stopping much.

07 · Kenya, 2015

Maasai Mara

I was 15. I spent time in the Mara learning under Peter Blackwell — a professional wildlife photographer and bush guide — and got my first real look at what a camera can do. The lions were in the grass. A baboon climbed an acacia tree to get away from them. A white rhino walked straight at our vehicle and didn't stop.

08 · Alabama · Gulf of Mexico

Home Water

Home water. The places I grew up fishing. Offshore runs for reef fish. Redfishing the flats on fly. The 40-inch bull redfish at the oil rig platform is still the biggest red I've ever touched. The aurora in May 2024 was something I'll never fully explain to someone who wasn't standing outside that night.
