New Zealand

Back Country

South Island, New Zealand, 2024. Fly fishing remote back-country rivers with water so clear you can count the rocks fifteen feet down. The Haast Pass, Pancake Rocks, and a New Year's painted in light over a mountain creek.

Vietnam

Ha Giang Loop

The Ha Giang Loop by motorbike through Vietnam's northern karst mountains. Ancient Hmong villages, switchback roads that drop into limestone gorges, and a river cave that felt like the end of the world.

Japan

Nara & Kyoto

Cherry blossom season in Nara and Kyoto. Deer wandering temple grounds like they own the place — because they do. Wagyu omakase in a twelve-seat counter restaurant where every plate was a sculpture.

Thailand

Chiang Mai

Northern Thailand at its slowest. Golden chedis and morning markets, bamboo bridges disappearing into hill country mist, and streets tangled with decades of wire.

Singapore

City After Dark

Singapore shot almost entirely after dark. The city earns its skyline — every building lit like it knows it's being watched.

Kenya

Maasai Mara

The Mara in dry season. Cheetah moving through tall grass, a baboon scrambling up an acacia to escape the lions at its base, and a white rhino walking straight at the vehicle like it owned the road.

American West

Wyoming & Utah

High desert fly fishing in Wyoming limestone canyons, chasing rainbow trout in water the color of glass. And Goblin Valley at dusk, where the hoodoos turn amber then purple as the sun drops.
