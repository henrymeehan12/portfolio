Japan — Attention

Japan · 2024

Japan rewards attention. Not the temples — though Fushimi Inari at dawn is everything — but the way Fuji appears between two buildings if you're looking at the right angle.

Tokyo at 4am is quiet in a way that makes no sense for a city of 14 million. The fish market is the loudest thing happening. Kyoto in cherry blossom season is almost too perfect — like someone designed it to be photographed.

I kept the camera up anyway.
