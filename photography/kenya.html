Maasai Mara

Maasai Mara · Kenya

I was 15. I spent time in the Mara learning under Peter Blackwell — a professional wildlife photographer and bush guide. He taught me what it means to wait for the right moment.

Not rushing. Not settling. The Mara has a logic to it once you stop imposing yours. You learn where the cats sleep in the afternoon. You learn which crossing point the wildebeest prefer. You wait there. The light changes. Something happens.

I came back a different photographer than I left.
