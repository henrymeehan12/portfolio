Yellowstone Drainage

Wyoming, USA

We packed a raft in to reach water that doesn't get fished. The hike in took most of a day. The meadows were flooded — you walked on submerged logs to reach the river. There were grizzly tracks in the sand that morning. Adult and cub.

Yellowstone cutthroat in the headwaters. Orange as a goldfish, fat as a football. The fish here don't see many flies. They're not selective — they're just present, fully, in a way that domesticated fish aren't.

The hike out nearly killed us. Worth it.
