01 · Montana · 2019–2024

The Madison River

Montana, USA

In 2017 I went on my first fly fishing trip to Montana with Lillard Fly Fishing Expeditions. I went back every summer. In 2019 Will Lillard asked if I'd guide for him.

Six seasons on the Madison River. The entomology curriculum — kick nets, macroinvertebrate sampling, matching insects to flies — runs through every day on the water. I've watched kids hold a fly rod for the first time and make a cast that a grown guide would be proud of.

The fawn walked up to me while I was rigging a rod on a gravel bar. I wasn't expecting it. I wasn't fishing anymore.
