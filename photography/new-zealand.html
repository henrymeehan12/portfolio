03 · New Zealand · Early 2024

Back Country

South Island · New Zealand

First stop on a four-month trip. I'd been planning this since 2017, after the first summer on the Madison. New Zealand back country fly fishing was what serious Montana guides talked about like it was a religion.

The rivers in the South Island are gin-clear and cold. No trails to get there. The fish had probably never seen a fly. Brown trout that will make you question whether you've actually been fishing your whole life or just practicing.

You carry everything in. You sleep on the ground. The trout here are beyond what the word "trout" prepares you for.
