Gulf Coast & Home

Alabama · Gulf of Mexico

Home water. Offshore for reef fish — snapper, grouper, amberjack over structure in the Gulf. Redfishing the grass flats on fly, which requires a completely different kind of patience than trout fishing. You're looking for the fish before you cast to it.

In May 2024 there was an aurora visible from the lake shore — not a rumor, not a social media thing, a real aurora over Alabama. I drove to the water and stayed until it faded.

Home has more going on than you realize until you start paying attention to it.
