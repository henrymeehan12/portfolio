Ha Giang & Beyond

Fiji · Vietnam · Singapore

After New Zealand: Fiji for two days offshore. Then Singapore, Thailand, and north into Vietnam. The Ha Giang Loop is four days of mountain roads in the far north — passes, karst towers, villages you couldn't find on most maps.

There's a cave on the Nho Que River where you take a boat in the dark and the stalactites come down to the water. I spent a long time in that cave. At one point on the road we stopped the bikes and a Hmong family waved us over to help hoe their corn. You don't plan for that. You just say yes.

The smiling kid ran up the road toward us like he'd been waiting all morning for something to happen.
